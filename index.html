The Role

What you'll be doing day to day

You'll work on complex, real-world data problems with tangible societal impact. This is a senior individual contributor role where you'll lead client engagements, architect solutions, and mentor those around you. No two days look the same.

Key Responsibilities

  • Lead client projects and provide subject matter expertise across data science and ML
  • Work in scrum-like environments for iterative, fail-fast innovation
  • Identify opportunities for data science to solve real client problems
  • Apply statistical and evidence-based techniques to drive insight
  • Design, evaluate, and implement on-premise, cloud and hybrid ML solutions
  • Build maintainable, production-grade code using modern data science libraries
  • Communicate complex findings clearly to both technical and non-technical audiences
  • Apply ethical principles throughout your approach to data

The Ideal Candidate

  • You enjoy complex data problems but love finding simple, elegant solutions
  • You're comfortable with ambiguity and driven by making things clearer
  • Clients love working with you — you're clear, trustworthy, and reliable
  • You can present to the boardroom as confidently as you can write production code
  • You're a genuine collaborator who steps up when it matters
  • You keep pace with a fast-evolving technology landscape
  • You think about the future and thrive on solving hard problems

What to Expect

A transparent look at the interview process

1

Initial Conversation

A relaxed call with your recruiter to explore fit, answer your questions, and give you an honest picture of the role.

2

Introductory Interview

A conversation with the Daintta team covering your background, experience, and how you approach data science problems.

3

Technical Assessment

You'll be given the opportunity to demonstrate your technical thinking — expect real-world, relevant scenarios rather than abstract puzzles.

4

Final Stage

A chance to meet more of the team, ask deeper questions, and make sure this is absolutely the right move for you.

A note on transparency: We believe the interview process should work both ways. At every stage, we encourage you to ask questions. By the time you reach an offer, you should feel completely confident in your decision. Nothing will surprise you.

Security Clearance

Important eligibility information

🛡️

SC Clearance Required

Due to the nature of this role, you will need to be willing and eligible to achieve a minimum of SC (Security Check) clearance. To qualify, you must be a British Citizen and have resided in the UK for the last five years. If you're unsure about your eligibility, you can find detailed information at gov.uk/government/organisations/united-kingdom-security-vetting. We're happy to talk this through with you openly before you apply.
